Welcome to Our ABC restaurant!

You can't eat here just once



Three decades ago, a small sweet shop was started by Late K.S. Arun raj, in the metropolis of Chennai. This same shop has now flowered into multiple branches that dot the length and breadth of Chennai as well as its neighbouring cities.
M/s. Sakthi Sweets & Snacks is now a well-established brand in Southern India that continues to grow with a vision to capture the global market. The baton of this sweet legacy was passed on by K.S. Arun raj to his sons, Mr. K.T. Venkatesan and Mr. K.T. Srinivasa Raja who now act as the driving force behind ABC.
